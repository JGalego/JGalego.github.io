## [Defect Detection with Amazon Lookout for Vision](https://medium.com/@joao.galego/defect-detection-with-amazon-lookout-for-vision-58184badfcb5)

At [re:Invent](https://reinvent.awsevents.com/), AWS unveiled a suite of industrial-focused products and services designed to accelerate digital transformation for manufacturing partners.

These offerings, branded as [AWS for Industrial](https://aws.amazon.com/industrial/), span from dedicated services like [Amazon Lookout for Equipment](https://aws.amazon.com/lookout-for-equipment/) and hardware devices ([Amazon Monitron](https://aws.amazon.com/monitron/)) to comprehensive solutions like [Amazon SageMaker for Predictive Maintenance](https://aws.amazon.com/solutions/implementations/predictive-maintenance-using-machine-learning/) — tackling real-world challenges in *Production & Asset Optimization* and *Quality Management*.

<!-- excerpt-start -->

This article introduces [AWS for Industrial](https://aws.amazon.com/industrial/) with a deep dive into [Amazon Lookout for Vision](https://aws.amazon.com/lookout-for-vision/), a service that transforms [defect detection](https://beyondminds.ai/blog/the-future-of-defect-detection-is-in-ai/) into straightforward binary image classification.
