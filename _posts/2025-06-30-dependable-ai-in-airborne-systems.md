## Dependable AI in Airborne Systems ✈️

<!-- excerpt-start -->

Integrating AI into safety-critical airborne systems presents profound challenges — this talk addresses the complexities of ensuring reliability, predictability, and compliance with stringent aviation safety standards.

<!-- excerpt-end -->

I'll discuss Critical Software's contributions to the Safe ONNX and WG-114 / SAEG34 (AI in Aviation) working groups, highlighting our work to standardize and certify AI models for safe aviation deployment.

Attendees will gain practical insights into overcoming AI integration hurdles in high-stakes environments.
