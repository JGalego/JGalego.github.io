João Galego

PhD Candidate | DevOps Engineer @ Siemens Lx Tech Hub

Born and raised in Lisbon (Portugal), I graduated in 2013 with a MSc degree in Physics from Instituto Superior Técnico - the best school of engineering in the country. During my senior year, I was offered a grant for undergraduate students to study at the Center for Theoretical Particle Physics (CFTP), where I did my MSc thesis work under the supervision of Prof. Gustavo Castelo Branco (prémio Gulbenkian de Ciência 2001, comendador da Ordem de Sant'Iago de Espada 2005, Humboldt Research Award 2005, Seed of Science 2009). Since then I've worked as a data analyst at Kantar Worldpanel - one of the leading Market Research companies in the world - and as a software tester and test automation engineer at Coriant - formerly part of Nokia Siemens Networks - and as a DevOps engineer at WinTrust / Innowave. In 2016, I decided to return to academia and completed a post-graduate degree course in Forensic Studies from Instituto Superior de Ciências Sociais e Políticas (ISCSP/UL). Currently, I'm working as a DevOps engineer at Siemens while pursuing a PhD in Cognitive Science at the University of Lisbon, where my dissertation will focus on the application of machine learning algorithms to improve the performance of EEG-based brain-computer interfaces.
